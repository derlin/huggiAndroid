Hugginess

"Encouraging Interpersonal Touch through Smart Clothes"

Context

This application is the result of a semester project at the University of Applied Sciences and Arts Western Switzerland Fribourg. Based on the Hugginess original idea, the student had to implement a first prototype, including a t-shirt and an application.

Concept Overview

Physical contact has an important role in human well-being.
Hugginess is a concept of interactive wearable system that encourages people to hug by augmenting this gesture with digital information exchange.

The system is based upon a smart t-shirt that recognizes hug and transmits digital information through conductive fabric while hugging. Thoes HuggiShirt can work standalone. Nevertheless, important and useful applications arise when they are paired to a smartphone or a PC.
Indeed, applications could monitor the user's daily interpersonal touch interactions, detect their lack and take proactive measure. For example, we could imagine an application location aware which would automatically send an SMS to a nearby friend.

See the original publication paper for more details.
